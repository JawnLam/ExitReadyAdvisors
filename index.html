Manufacturing Firm Boosts Sale Price by 45%

Industry: Specialized Manufacturing | Revenue: $8M

The Challenge:

The business was heavily reliant on the owner's personal relationships with suppliers and clients. Financials were messy, and there were no documented operational procedures.

The Solution:

Over 18 months, we implemented a new ERP system, created a standard operating procedures manual, and transitioned key client relationships to a newly empowered sales manager.

The Result:

Final sale price was 45% higher than the initial broker estimate, with three competing offers.

Service Business Reduces Owner Dependency

Industry: B2B Services | Revenue: $12M

The Challenge:

The owner was working 70+ hours a week and was the sole decision-maker. Buyers saw this as a massive risk, leading to low initial interest.

The Solution:

We established a leadership team, delegated P&L responsibility to department heads, and implemented a management incentive plan tied to company performance.

The Result:

The business was sold as a "turnkey" operation, attracting a strategic buyer who paid a 2.5x multiple premium.
