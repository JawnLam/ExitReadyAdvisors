Notes to Balance Sheet - Construction Industry Specific
Note 1: Basis of Presentation and Revenue Recognition (ASC 606)
The company recognizes revenue on long-term construction contracts using the percentage-of-completion method, measured by the cost-to-cost method. This results in contract assets (unbilled revenue) when costs incurred plus recognized profits exceed billings, and contract liabilities (billings in excess of costs) when the opposite occurs. The net contract asset position of $40,000 reflects the company's balanced approach to progress billings.
